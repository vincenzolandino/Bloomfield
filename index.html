About Bloomfield

Bloomfield is a custom AI software company that builds operational intelligence tools for manufacturers. Founded by Vincenzo Landino, Bloomfield helps manufacturers reduce quoting errors, capture institutional knowledge from retiring workers, and improve on-time delivery through custom AI-powered software built around how each operation actually runs.

Bloomfield offers four core solutions: Quoting Tools for compressing RFQ turnaround from days to hours, Knowledge Engine for preserving tribal knowledge and process expertise in searchable systems, Production Pulse for real-time delivery risk visibility across ERP and floor data, and Equipment Intelligence for surfacing machine utilization and maintenance patterns.

Bloomfield serves precision machining, aerospace, defense, hydraulics, metal fabrication, CNC manufacturing, fluid power, medical device, and industrial component manufacturers across the United States. The company engages through two models: Advisory and Strategy for AI readiness assessments and workflow audits, and Custom Build for end-to-end development of AI software integrated with existing ERP and operational systems.

Bloomfield was founded in 2026 and is headquartered in Connecticut. The company website is https://getbloomfield.com.

Fig. 4 — Process

How an engagement works, start to finish

01
Listen
We spend time with the people who do the work. Estimators, engineers, production managers, floor supervisors. We ask where their time goes, what slows them down, and what information they wish they had at their fingertips. The bottleneck is usually obvious to the team living with it every day.
02
Diagnose
We trace the bottleneck back to its source. That means mapping every system, handoff, and decision point in the workflow. Where does an estimator go to find pricing history? How many people does a scheduler talk to before confirming a ship date? The diagnosis is specific enough to act on.
03
Structure
Most manufacturing data already exists. It is just scattered across ERPs, spreadsheets, shared drives, email threads, and the notes taped to a machine. We pull it together, clean it, and organize it into something an intelligent system can actually use.
04
Build
We deliver working software in phases. Not a proof of concept that sits in a slide deck. A tool your team can use on day one of delivery, shaped around the specific workflow we diagnosed. Quoting systems, knowledge bases, dashboards, agents.
05
Refine
The first version is never the last. Once your team starts using the tool, we watch what works, what gets ignored, and what they wish it did differently. We iterate until the tool fits the work like it was always there.
